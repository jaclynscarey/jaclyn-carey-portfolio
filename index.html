Trivia Game - Are You Smarter Than ChatGPT???

The topics of the questions range from math, science, history, geography, literature, and 90's movies. Questions are randomly selected from a pool of 106 questions with each new game. The game's layout is responsive, which makes it possible to be played on desktop or mobile devices.
